PROJECT DESCRIPTION

Once you have a construction project you would wish our company to do for you, the following are the description of how the project will be done:

  1. Contact us or visit our nearest company branch near your location
  2. Explain to our Engineering solution team on your project and they will be ready to listen to you
  3. Sign an agreement contract with our company
  4. Pay a required amount of deposit as you have agreed with our company
  5. Produce a copy of your national Identity card, land Title deed, Construction permit from the locl authority and the presise location of your project
  6. The duration of the project will depend on the project magnitude but with our qualified staff we will complete in the shortest time possible. Quality is always our priority
