WELCOME TO OUR WEBSITE

We are capable engineering contractors located in Kenya. We have been in service since the year 2012. We have different engineers specializing in the different fields of engineering. Our engineers as well as our firm is certified and recognized by the Engineering Board of Kenya. We visualise having strong and durable engineering structures equipped with the modern technology in the different parts of Kenya. What Uwezo Engineering Solutions Limited (UESL) really offers is to have most if not all the engineering sections under one roof. This is with a goal of making it easy for our customers get reach of a reliable company which we fulfill its engineering projects' needs.
