MOTTO

YOU NEED IT WE FIX IT!

Mision

What really our mission is here at Uwezo contracting company is to be efficient as possible with what we do. We aim at being reliable partners with our clients as we walk through solving the engineering problems they have. On that note, we also aim at keeping our backyard clean through employing credible engineers who are up to the task and appreciate business ethics. Moreover, we believe that a happy employee is a happy client. Therefore, in endeavours, we try as much as possible to make our engineers comfortable in the work stations.

In addition, giving back to the community is a duty that we appreciate here. We have done several contributions to the in our region through programmes we have initiated. We construct non-profit homes for the homeless and fund their day to day running among other charitable projects we indulge in.

Vision

We as Uwezo Engineering Solutions Limited we are hopeful and visionary as we perform our services. In 5 years we see ourselves having spread our roots. Apart from the 3 major cities of Kenya we have covered so far, we expect to potential buyers in major towns such as Nakuru and Eldoret and counties such as Kiambu. Preparations are in progress to getting there.
